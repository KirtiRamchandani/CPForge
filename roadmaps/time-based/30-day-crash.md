# 30-Day Crash Course

High-intensity prep when interview date is fixed.

## Daily (2–3 hours)
| Block | Time | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| Warmup | 20m | 1 easy review |
| Main | 70m | 2 mediums (one weak topic) |
| Review | 20m | Spaced repetition |
| Log | 10m | Mistake + tomorrow plan |

## Week 1: Arrays & strings
- Two pointers, sliding window, prefix sums
- `cp-forge sheet --topic arrays --limit 20`

## Week 2: Trees & graphs
- BFS/DFS, shortest path basics
- `cp-forge sheet --topic graphs --limit 15`

## Week 3: DP & heap
- 1D DP, top-K, intervals
- `cp-forge doctor` mid-week check

## Week 4: Mocks & company focus
- `cp-forge roadmap --company <target> --days 7`
- Full timed mocks every other day

```bash
cp-forge launch --goal interview --days 30
cp-forge today
cp-forge review today
```
